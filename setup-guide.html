Getting Started

Your Setup Guide.
Everything in One Place.

Follow these simple steps to forward your calls to Ari and connect your calendar. You'll be live in minutes.

⚠️
iPhone Users — Do This First

Before setting up call forwarding, go to Settings → Phone → Live Voicemail and toggle it OFF. If Live Voicemail is on, it will intercept your calls before they reach Ari. You only need to do this once.

Step 1

Set Up Call Forwarding

The best setup for any business owner — your phone rings first. If you answer, you handle it. If you don't, Ari picks it up. You never miss a call either way.

⭐
Recommended Setup for Every Business Owner
Conditional Call Forwarding — "Forward When Unanswered"
Your phone rings first like normal. If you pick up — great, you handle it. If you don't answer within a few rings, Ari automatically takes over and makes sure no opportunity is lost. You stay in control. Nothing falls through the cracks.
🍎
iPhone (iOS)
💡 iPhone Tip: The Settings app only supports "forward ALL calls." For the recommended conditional setup, use the dial code below instead — it's quick and works on every carrier.

✅ Recommended — Dial Code Method:

  1. 1
    Open your Phone app and go to the keypad
  2. 2
    Dial *61* then your Ari number then #
    *61*8886096585#
  3. 3
    Press Call — you'll hear a confirmation tone
  4. 4
    Test it — call your number and let it ring. Ari should answer after a few rings
To turn off conditional forwarding:
#61#
⚠️ Before setting up, go to Settings → Phone → Live Voicemail and turn it OFF — or it will intercept calls before Ari can answer.
🤖
Android
💡 Android Tip: Android supports conditional forwarding directly in Settings — no dial code needed. Follow the steps below for your phone brand.

✅ Recommended — Settings Method:

  1. 1
    Open the Phone app
  2. 2
    Tap the three-dot menu → Settings
  3. 3
    Tap Calling Accounts → your SIM → Call Forwarding
    Samsung users: look for Supplementary Services
  4. 4
    Select "Forward when unanswered"
  5. 5
    Enter your Ari number and tap Turn On
  6. 6
    Test it — call your number and let it ring. Ari should answer
Or use dial code on any Android:
*61*8886096585#

FAQ

Common Setup Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from new customers.

Does my phone number change?
Not at all. Your customers keep calling the exact same number they always have. You simply add a forwarding setting in your phone — one change, takes about 60 seconds.
Will I still be able to make outgoing calls?
Yes — call forwarding only affects incoming calls. Your outgoing calls work exactly as normal.
What if I want to answer a call myself?
Set up conditional forwarding — "forward when unanswered" — and your phone rings first. If you pick up, you handle it. If you don't answer within a few rings, Ari takes over.
How will I know when Ari books an appointment?
You'll get an instant text and email notification after every call with the full details — caller name, number, what they need, and any appointment that was booked.
I don't see Call Forwarding in my iPhone settings. What do I do?
Some carriers hide this setting or don't support it on certain plans. Try the dial code method — dial *72 followed by your Ari number and press Call. If that doesn't work, contact your carrier and ask them to enable call forwarding on your account.
How do I turn off call forwarding?
Go back to the same settings where you turned it on and toggle it off. Or dial ##002# from your phone dialer — this turns off all forwarding on any phone and any carrier.
